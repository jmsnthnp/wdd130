Rafting Site Plan

James Nathan Pecundo

Overview

Purpose

"The purpose of our website plan is to establish a robust online presence for our rafting business, connecting thrill-seekers with unforgettable adventures, safety information, and seamless booking experiences, all while showcasing the natural wonders of our river journeys."

Audience

Target Customers: Our target customers for this website plan are outdoor enthusiasts and adventure seekers aged 25 to 45, with a moderate to comfortable financial situation. They are individuals and families who have a strong passion for outdoor activities, especially water rafting. They have disposable income for recreational adventures and are likely to travel to experience thrilling rafting trips. Their interests include exploring nature, seeking excitement, and creating memorable experiences with loved ones.

What They Want: They want access to information about exciting and safe white-water rafting experiences in diverse natural settings. They seek thrilling adventures that cater to various skill levels, from beginners to experienced rafters. Additionally, they want a seamless and secure online booking process that allows them to plan and reserve their trips conveniently.

What They Expect to Find: By accessing the website, they expect to find detailed descriptions of rafting trips, including information about river routes, difficulty levels, and the natural beauty of the surroundings. They anticipate clear safety guidelines, pricing transparency, and options for group bookings. They also want to read reviews and testimonials from previous rafters to gauge the quality of the experience. Overall, they look to the website to provide them with the knowledge and tools needed to plan an unforgettable rafting adventure.
